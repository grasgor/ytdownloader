========================================
  VIDEO DOWNLOADER - How to Use
========================================

1. Open the app by double-clicking VideoDownloader.exe

2. Copy the link to any video (YouTube, etc.)
   and click the [Paste] button — or paste it yourself.

3. Pick the quality you want from the dropdown:
     - Best available  → highest quality
     - 1080p / 720p / 480p / 360p → specific size
     - Audio only (mp3) → saves just the music/audio

4. Choose where to save it (defaults to your Downloads folder).

5. Click [Download] and wait for the green bar to finish.

That's it! The file will be in the folder you chose.

========================================
  NOTES
========================================
- First download may take a moment to start (normal).
- Make sure you're connected to the internet.
- Some videos may be age-restricted or region-locked
  and cannot be downloaded.
